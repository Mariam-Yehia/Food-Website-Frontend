Homemade hot chocolate



Indulge in a homemade hot chocolate. Perfect as the cold nights draw in, you can add a pinch of chilli, cinnamon or a slug of rum if you like


5 mins 5mins easy

Nutrition per serving

kcal fat saturates carbs sugars fibre protein salt
438 22g 14g 47g 0g 2g 12g 0.3g

Ingredients


  • 250ml milk of your choice

  • 1 tbsp cocoa

  • 1-2 tbsp soft light brown sugar

  • 25g dark or plain chocolate, finely chopped, plus a grating to serve

  • 1 tbsp whipped cream or squirty cream

Way to cook


  1. Heat the milk, cocoa, sugar and chocolate in a small pan over a medium heat until steaming and the chocolate has melted. Whisk to dissolve the cocoa.

  2. Pour into a mug, then add the cream to float on top. Grate over a little more chocolate to serve.
